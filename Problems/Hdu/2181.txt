<h1 style="color:#1A5CC8">哈密顿绕行世界问题</h1>
<font><b><span style="font-family:Arial;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;color:green">Time Limit: 3000/1000 MS (Java/Others)&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;Memory Limit: 32768/32768 K (Java/Others)<br>Total Submission(s): 1272&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;Accepted Submission(s): 793<br></span></b></font><br><br><div class="panel_title" align="left">Problem Description</div> <div class="panel_content">一个规则的实心十二面体，它的 20个顶点标出世界著名的20个城市，你从一个城市出发经过每个城市刚好一次后回到出发的城市。 <br>
</div>
